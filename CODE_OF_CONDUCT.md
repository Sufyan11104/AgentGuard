# Code Of Conduct

AgentGuard follows a simple contributor standard: be professional, constructive, and respectful.

## Expected Behavior

- Keep discussion focused on the project and its defensive security goals.
- Treat other contributors with respect.
- Give actionable feedback.
- Respect safety boundaries around AI security content.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Sharing real secrets, stolen data, malware, phishing, or third-party attack instructions.
- Encouraging misuse of AgentGuard or its test content.
- Deliberately derailing technical discussions.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove content, close issues, block users, or reject contributions that violate this code of conduct or the safety policy.
